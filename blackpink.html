Blackpink (Korean: 블랙핑크; commonly stylized as BLACKPINK or BLΛƆKPIИK) is a South Korean girl group that debuted on 8 August 2016. The group consists of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They are the highest-charting female K-pop act on both Billboard Hot 100 and Billboard 200.

THE ALBUM

The Album (stylized in all uppercase and alternatively titled "BLΛƆKPIИK The Album") is the first Korean studio album and second album overall by BLACKPINK.
